Algorithm Benchmarking App

Background

As a programmer you must be familiar with that gut feeling that this second nested loop you're just making is going to slow down your application.

But what about that third loop? And that fourth loop? Recursive calls? HA!

Although there's plenty of resources that explain what's the Big O notation and what are the most efficient algorithms sometimes the little skeptical on us comes back at us saying "Do you really believe it?"

This is my approach to show you that you don't have to be skeptical. You can just try it yourself, in your own browser.

How this works

Pretty simple:

  1. Click on Setup
  2. Select the algorithms you want to test, the amount of repetitions of each algorithm and the size of the collection to sort.
  3. Click on Benchmark
  4. Click RUN
  5. ???
  6. Profit!

You will see how the graph automatically populates and provides you with a neat visualization you can then filter or zoom to verify your results.

Wanna know more?

Click on Resources to get a list of nifty resources to learn more about Big O notation or the sources of the algorithms that were used during the making of this site.
